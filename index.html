How does it work?

Money on your account in just 5 minutes!

  • 1
    Download Zenka App

    Visit Google Play Store. Search for Zenka Loan App and download it on your mobile.

  • 2
    Fill the application form

    After downloading our app, choose how much money you need and sign up with pesapawa.

  • 3
    Receive money on your M-Pesa

    Your application will be evaluated and when it is approved, you will receive money on your M-Pesa

  • 4
    Repay on time and build a positive credit history

    Timely repayment increases your credit limit and allows you to apply for higher amounts in the future.
